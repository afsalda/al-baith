# Apartment

## Overview
Fully furnished apartments designed for families and long-term stays with complete home-style facilities.

## Apartment Types

### 1 BHK Apartment
- **Capacity**: Up to 3 Guests
- **Pricing**: ₹3,500 per night
- **Features**: 1 Bedroom, 1 King Bed, Attached Bathroom, Kitchen, Living Area.
![1 BHK Apartment](/images/apartment/apt-1.jpg)

### 2 BHK Apartment
- **Capacity**: Up to 5 Guests
- **Pricing**: ₹5,500 per night
- **Features**: 2 Bedrooms, 2 King Beds, 2 Bathrooms, Full Kitchen, Spacious Living.
![2 BHK Apartment](/images/apartment/apt-2.jpg)

### 3 BHK Apartment
- **Capacity**: Up to 8 Guests
- **Pricing**: ₹7,500 per night
- **Features**: 3 Bedrooms, 3 King Beds, 3 Bathrooms, Full Kitchen & Dining, Elegant Living.
![3 BHK Apartment](/images/apartment/apt-3.jpg)

## Common Features
- Separate bedrooms
- Attached bathroom for every bedroom
- Living area
- Full kitchen
- Air Conditioning
- Free Wi-Fi
- Television
- Heater
- Power Backup
